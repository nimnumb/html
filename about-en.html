about


My name is Nimrod Markovich and I am a linguist from Israel. I am a freelance linguist and I work with companies from abroad in a variety of projects that require translation and localization, phonemic transcription, annotation and labeling data, editing, proofreading (including Hebrew diacritics and vocalization), quality control (QC) of subtitles and dubbed audios (Hebrew) of mainstream TV shows and movies, and more.
For as long as I can remember, I have always loved languages. As a child I heard about a special language called Esperanto, which intrigued me a lot, and in 2015 I started learning it. This language is not an ordinary language like the other languages, as it is a constructed, neutral language, that was invented in order to create equal communication between speakers of different languages, and to create equality between different ethnic groups. I decided to build a website that is entirely dedicated to this special language, and here is the result!
So I hope you'll like it!
Have fun!
